# SECTION 5: Guidance & Tips

### Special Considerations for Korean Clients
- **Dining**: Korean meal options available at all locations (LA Koreatown, LV Korean restaurants)
- **Language**: Korean interpretation provided throughout
- **Payment**: KakaoPay international payment guidance, USD currency tips
- **Golf**: Club rental pre-booking ($60–120/set)
- **Tipping Culture**: US tipping guide (dining 18–22%, hotel bellhop $5–10, caddie $40–60)

### Seasonal Recommendations
- **Mar–May**: Optimal. 18–28°C (64–82°F), course conditions at their best
- **Jun–Aug**: Palm Springs/LV extreme heat (40°C+ / 104°F+). Early morning tee times recommended
- **Sep–Nov**: Second optimal window. Wine harvest season
- **Dec–Feb**: LA/San Diego pleasant, LV/Palm Springs mild
