## 1. Executive Summary

### Business Overview

**Resonate Tour (결 투어)** is a premium California golf tour package business targeting Korean golfers. It operates through two websites:

| Site | Domain | Primary Function | Target Market |
|------|--------|------------------|---------------|
| **Resonate Tour** | resonatetour.com | California golf 13-day packages, scorecards, schedules | Korean golfers → USA |
| **Resonate Club** | resonateclub.com | Korea inbound packages (luxury/standard), USA golf tours (Korean) | Bidirectional travelers |

### Key Insight

**The Korean outbound golf tourism market is experiencing explosive growth.** Annual overseas golf spending approximates $2 billion. Seoul's average green fee ($171) is roughly double Los Angeles ($90), creating a powerful economic driver. The perception that **"one round in Korea = an entire golf trip abroad"** has fueled massive growth in golf travel to China (Guangzhou), Japan, and Southeast Asia. Korean golf bookings to Guangzhou surged **+300% YoY in 2024**.

**California remains a largely untapped blue ocean.** Korean golfers predominantly travel to Japan, China, and Southeast Asia. Very few Korea-based travel agencies specialize in California golf tours. This represents a significant **first-mover advantage opportunity**.

### Recommended Business Structure

**Korea + US dual-entity structure** — Korea handles marketing, client relationships, and KRW payments; the US handles vendor contracts, operations, and risk management:

```
[Korean Yuhan Hoesa] ← KRW payments, client management, marketing
        ↕ (Service Agreement)
[US Wyoming LLC] ← USD payments, golf course/hotel/transport contracts
```

## 3. Business Structure & Legal Requirements

### 3.1 Korean Entity Formation (F-4 Visa)

**F-4 (Overseas Korean) visa holders can freely establish businesses.** The only restriction is on "simple manual labor" sectors — business ownership and management is fully permitted.

#### Recommended: Yuhan Hoesa (유한회사, Limited Company)

A Yuhan Hoesa offers simpler formation than a Chusik Hoesa (stock company), has no minimum capital requirement, and no mandatory auditor.

**Formation Process (startbiz.go.kr):**

1. **Search and reserve company name** — via startbiz.go.kr
2. **Draft and notarize Articles of Incorporation** — through a law firm (~₩100,000–200,000)
3. **Deposit capital and obtain bank certificate** — capital deposit certificate
4. **Register at court registry** — registration tax: 0.48% of capital (including local education tax)
5. **Business registration** — via tax office or Hometax online
6. **Social insurance enrollment** — 4 major insurances (pension, health, employment, industrial accident)
7. **Notify immigration** — within 14 days of business commencement (HiKorea)

| Item | Yuhan Hoesa | Individual Proprietor |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| Minimum capital | None | None |
| Liability scope | Limited to investment | Unlimited |
| Corporate tax rate | 9% (≤₩200M) / 19% (₩200M–20B) | 6–45% progressive |
| Formation cost | ~₩500K–1M | Free |
| Credibility | High (B2B advantage) | Medium |
| **Recommendation** | ✅ **Strongly recommended** | Testing phase only |

#### Tax Summary

| Tax Type | Rate | Filing Cycle |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Corporate Income Tax | 9% (≤₩200M) / 19% (₩200M–20B) | Annual |
| VAT | 10% | Quarterly |
| Local Income Tax | 10% of corporate tax | Annual |
| Withholding (payroll) | Earned income tax + 4 insurances | Monthly |

### 3.2 Travel Agency License (Overseas Travel Business)

A **Gukwoe Yeohaengeop (국외여행업, Overseas Travel Agency)** license is mandatory under Article 4 of the Tourism Promotion Act for sending Korean tourists abroad.

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Capital | **₩40 million minimum** (corporation) |
| Business Bond/Insurance | **₩80 million** (Seoul Guarantee Insurance or Cooperative guarantee) |
| Physical Office | Required (no virtual offices) |
| Qualified Staff | Minimum **1 person** (tourism interpreter, overseas tour conductor, or tourism major) |
| Registration Authority | City/Province Tourism Department |
| Processing Time | 2–4 weeks |

> **F-4 visa holders ARE eligible for this license.** No nationality restriction for travel business ownership.

### 3.3 US Entity Design

#### Recommended: Wyoming LLC + S-Corp Election

**Why Wyoming LLC:**
- Low formation cost ($100)
- No state income tax
- Strong privacy protections
- Charging Order Protection (asset protection)
- Non-residents can form

**Why S-Corp Election:**
- Pay reasonable salary (subject to payroll taxes)
- Remaining profits = distributions (no 15.3% self-employment tax)
- **Significant savings when profits exceed salary by $60K+**
- **Must be US citizen/resident** — you qualify as Korean-American

**Formation Steps:**

1. File Wyoming Articles of Organization — **$100**
2. Appoint Registered Agent — **~$125/year** (Northwest, LegalZoom, etc.)
3. Obtain **EIN from IRS** — free, instant online
4. Create Operating Agreement
5. Open US business bank account (Mercury, Relay — foreign owner friendly)
6. File **BOI Report** with FinCEN (Corporate Transparency Act)
7. **Register as California Seller of Travel** — $100/year (CA Attorney General)

#### ⚠️ California Seller of Travel (Mandatory)

Per CA Attorney General (oag.ca.gov/travel):
- Registration fee: $100/year
- Registration number must appear on all advertising
- Trust account or bond required for client funds
- Renew annually

### 4.3 Pricing Strategy

#### Core Principles

1. **Transparent pricing:** Replace "contact for price" with detailed published rates (builds trust)
2. **FX fluctuation response:** Price adjustment clause when USD/KRW moves ±5%
3. **Early bird discount:** 10% off for 90-day advance booking → improves cash flow
4. **Group discount:** 5% for 4+, 8% for 8+ → attract groups
5. **Repeat customer discount:** 7% for returning clients → maximize LTV

#### Pricing Psychology

| Technique | Application | Effect |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Decoy Effect | Signature ($14,900) + Classic ($8,500) + Light ($4,500) | Nudge toward Classic |
| Charm Pricing | $8,490 (not $8,500) | Perceived deal |
| Anchoring | "Pebble Beach solo $595 → $200 in package" | Value perception |
| Scarcity | "Maximum 2 groups per month" | Creates urgency |
