## 4. Golf Course Deep Dive

### 4.1 Trump National Golf Club Los Angeles

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Location** | Rancho Palos Verdes, CA (40 min by car from LAX) |
| **Designers** | Pete Dye (original), Donald Trump / Tom Fazio (redesign) |
| **Par / Length** | Par 71 / 7,242 yards (championship tees) |
| **Course Type** | Links-style + Cliffside (ocean bluff course) |
| **Ranking** | Golf Digest **Top 100 Public Courses in America** |
| **Signature** | Pacific Ocean views from every hole; Catalina Island on the horizon |
| **Difficulty** | Slope Rating 135–148 (tee-dependent), recommended for intermediate-advanced |

**Course Character:**

Pete Dye's strategic brilliance shines across this layout, perched along the Palos Verdes Peninsula coastal bluffs. Often called the **"Pebble Beach of the West,"** this course demands attention less for the scorecard and more for the sheer spectacle — tee shots launched toward the Pacific, sea breezes skimming the cliffs, and unforgettable sunsets.

- **Front Nine:** Expansive ocean-view holes tracing the upper bluffs. Beware of marine layer (June Gloom).
- **Back Nine:** Turns inland with strategic bunkering and undulating greens that raise the difficulty.
- **Hole 15 (Par 3):** The course signature — the Pacific directly beneath your feet; 180-yard downhill shot.
- **Hole 18 (Par 4):** A dramatic finishing hole crossing a waterfall-fed pond; clubhouse gallery applause awaits.

**Best Season:** April–June, September–October (20–25°C, minimal rainfall, low marine layer probability)

**⚠️ Advisory:**
- Morning tee times may face reduced visibility from marine layer → recommend tee-off after 9:00 AM
- Coastal cliff winds significantly affect ball flight — allow 2+ clubs of adjustment
- Caddie reservations required at least 1 week in advance (limited availability)

### 4.2 Hidden Valley Golf Club

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Location** | Norco, CA (Riverside County, ~1 hr from downtown LA) |
| **Designer** | Casey O'Callaghan |
| **Par / Length** | Par 72 / 6,800+ yards (championship tees) |
| **Course Type** | Mountain Valley |
| **Signature** | Dramatic elevation changes, Sierra Madre mountain panoramas |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate-advanced — demands elevation management and strategic shot-making |

**Course Character:**

If Trump National is the open freedom of the coast, Hidden Valley delivers the **epitome of mountain golf**. Casey O'Callaghan maximized the Inland Empire's rolling terrain to design a course where golf balls seem to soar across canyons — delivering adrenaline-pumping tee shot experiences.

- **Elevation:** Multiple holes boast drops exceeding 100 feet (30m) — exhilarating even from the cart
- **Strategy:** Blind shots, two-tier greens, natural rock hazards — O'Callaghan's trademark challenges
- **Views:** Sierra Madre mountain range and Inland Empire valley panoramas — autumn's golden landscape is breathtaking
- **Seasonal Highlight:** Spring (March–May) wildflower bloom is spectacular

**Best Season:** March–May (wildflowers), October–November (fall colors, crisp temperatures)

**⚠️ Advisory:**
- Summer (July–August) can exceed 35°C — recommend tee-off before 8:00 AM
- Cart-only course with steep sections — assess senior guests' comfort in advance
- Green speeds vary significantly by season — always check the practice green before the round

## 5. Catalina Island Exclusive Experience

### Overview

Located 22 miles (35 km) off the California mainland, **Catalina Island** is a hidden gem even most LA locals have never visited. Standard tourists endure a 1+ hour ferry ride; Resonate Tour guests arrive via **dedicated helicopter in 20 minutes**. With 88% of the island designated as a nature preserve, Catalina offers pristine natural beauty and a unique ecosystem.

### Experience Details

**① Airbus H125 Chartered Helicopter (Round-Trip)**

- LA mainland (Palos Verdes helipad) ↔ Catalina Avalon, 20 minutes each way
- Low-altitude flight with coastal cliff, Santa Monica Bay, and Catalina Channel scenery
- 6-passenger capacity, window seats for all, noise-canceling headsets
- **Weather Cancellation Policy:** Fog or high winds trigger same-day cancellation → ferry backup option available (1 hour transit)

**② Private Yacht Tour (2 Hours)**

- Depart Avalon Harbor, cruise the Catalina coastline
- Dolphin, seal, and seasonal whale sightings (probability varies by season)
- Champagne and canapé service, snorkeling gear provided
- Maximum 6 passengers, dedicated captain + crew

**③ Lovers Cove Snorkeling**

- Catalina Marine Preserve underwater ecosystem experience
- Garibaldi (California's state marine fish, vivid orange), giant kelp forests
- Water temperature: summer 20–22°C, winter 15–16°C (wetsuits provided)
- Beginner-friendly shallow depths (average 2–5m)

**④ Jeep Eco-Tour (2 Hours)**

- 4WD open Jeep exploration of Catalina's interior nature preserve
- **Bison Herd Observation:** Descendants of bison left on the island after 1924 film production — approximately 150 individuals today
- Catalina Island Fox, bald eagles, and other endemic species
- English-speaking naturalist guide

**⑤ VIP Casino Tour**

- Avalon Casino Building — 1929 Art Deco architectural masterpiece
- Ground floor: Movie theater (one of the oldest continuously operating theaters in America)
- Upper floor: Ballroom (55m-diameter circular dance hall, 6,000-person capacity)
- VIP access to areas closed to the general public

**⑥ Descanso Beach Club Cabana**

- Private cabana rental at Descanso Beach Club
- Signature Mai Tai cocktails, seafood platters
- Optional add-on activities: jet skis, kayaking
